Who We Are

Our Story

FurEver Home started as a small community initiative with a big heart. Our founders — a group of cat lovers, rescuers, and volunteers — came together to make sure every feline gets a second chance at a happy life. What began in a single garage has now grown into a recognized platform connecting hundreds of stray and abandoned cats to loving families.

Our Mission

We are committed to rescuing cats in need, advocating for responsible pet ownership, and building a community where no cat is left behind. Every whisker counts.
