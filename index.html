Adoption Fees

  • Cats: RM80 (includes vaccination & deworming)
  • Kittens: RM100 (includes vaccination & deworming)
  • Dogs: RM120 (includes vaccination, microchip & license)

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring when adopting a pet?

You’ll need to bring your identification and payment. We also recommend bringing a carrier or leash.

Are the animals vaccinated?

Yes. All animals are vaccinated, dewormed, and medically checked before adoption.

Can I adopt more than one animal?

Yes, depending on availability and your home readiness assessment.
